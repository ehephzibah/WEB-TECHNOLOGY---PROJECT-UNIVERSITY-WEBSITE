Accessibility

Inclusive Digital Experience

NovaTech Institute is committed to providing an accessible, inclusive, and user-friendly digital experience for every visitor. We continually improve our website to ensure equal access regardless of ability or technology.

Our Commitment

Accessibility is a core part of our digital strategy. We strive to design and maintain a website that is intuitive, inclusive, and accessible to students, parents, faculty, researchers, and visitors from around the world.

Website Features

Our website includes features designed to improve accessibility, including:

  • Keyboard-friendly navigation
  • Responsive layouts for all devices
  • High-contrast interface elements
  • Readable typography and spacing
  • Meaningful page headings
  • Alternative text for informative images
  • Accessible forms and navigation menus

Browser Compatibility

NovaTech's website is regularly tested on modern web browsers, including Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Mozilla Firefox, and Safari, ensuring a consistent experience across desktop and mobile platforms.

Continuous Improvement

Accessibility is an ongoing process. We regularly review our website, identify areas for improvement, and adopt modern web standards to enhance usability for everyone.
